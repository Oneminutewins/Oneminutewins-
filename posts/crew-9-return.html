Crew-9 Returns to Earth After 286 Days in Space!

NASA astronauts Sunita Williams, Butch Wilmore, Nick Hague, and cosmonaut Aleksandr Gorbunov safely splashed down off Florida’s coast on March 18, 2025, after a 286-day mission aboard the ISS. The SpaceX Dragon capsule landed at 5:57 PM EST, marking the end of a dramatic journey delayed by Boeing Starliner issues.
