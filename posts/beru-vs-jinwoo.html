Beru vs. Jinwoo in Solo Leveling

In *Solo Leveling*, the battle between Beru, the Ant King, and Jinwoo Sung is a turning point! Beru, a monstrous S-rank shadow, challenges Jinwoo after being revived as his loyal soldier. Their fight showcases Jinwoo’s growth as the Shadow Monarch, with Beru’s immense strength pushing him to his limits. Ultimately, Jinwoo’s strategic mind and necromancer powers win out, solidifying Beru as a key ally. This epic clash is a fan-favorite moment in the series!
