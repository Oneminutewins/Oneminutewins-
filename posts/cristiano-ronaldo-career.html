Career of Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo’s career is legendary! Starting at Sporting CP, he rose to fame at Manchester United, winning his first Ballon d’Or in 2008. At Real Madrid, he scored 450 goals in 438 games, securing four more Ballon d’Ors. After stints at Juventus and a return to United, he joined Al-Nassr in 2023, where he’s still breaking records at 40. With over 900 career goals and 5 Champions League titles, Ronaldo’s work ethic and skill are unmatched!
