Neuralink Files ‘Telepathy’ and ‘Telekinesis’ Trademarks

Elon Musk’s brain implant company, Neuralink, has filed trademark applications for futuristic terms like "Telepathy" and "Telekinesis" in 2025. These filings hint at ambitious plans to expand beyond its current brain-computer interface technology, which already allows paralyzed patients to control devices with their minds.

The "Telepathy" trademark suggests a future where Neuralink could enable direct brain-to-brain communication, while "Telekinesis" implies potential control of physical objects through thought. The company’s recent success, including a 2024 human trial where a patient played chess using neural signals, supports these bold visions. Neuralink aims to treat neurological conditions and eventually merge human cognition with AI.

Regulatory approval and ethical debates will play a big role in these developments. If successful, Neuralink could redefine human capability—stay tuned for more on this groundbreaking tech!
